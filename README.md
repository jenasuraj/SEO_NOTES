# SEO Notes for Freshers

## What is SEO?
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving a website’s visibility on search engines like Google.

---

## Types of SEO

### 1. On-Page SEO
Optimizing content and elements on your website.

- Title tags
- Meta descriptions
- Headings (H1, H2, H3)
- URL structure
- Internal linking
- Image optimization (alt text)

---

### 2. Off-Page SEO
Actions taken outside your website.

- Backlinks
- Social sharing
- Guest posting

---

### 3. Technical SEO
Improving backend structure.

- Website speed
- Mobile responsiveness
- Sitemap
- Robots.txt
- Crawling & Indexing

---

## Keyword Research

### What is a Keyword?
A word or phrase users search on Google.

### Types:
- Short-tail: "shoes"
- Long-tail: "best running shoes for men"

### Tips:
- Use relevant keywords
- Avoid keyword stuffing
- Focus on user intent

---

## Meta Tags

### Title Tag
- Keep under 60 characters
- Include main keyword

**Example:**
Good: Buy Running Shoes Online | Free Delivery

---

### Meta Description
- Keep under 160 characters
- Should be engaging
